Neocode ("we", "our", or "the Company") values your privacy and complies with applicable data protection laws. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it while providing our mobile game application ("Service").

1. Information We Collect

  • Device Information: device model, OS version, unique identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID, IDFA)
  • User Identifiers: in-game user ID, nickname, and login account details (Google, Apple ID)
  • Purchase Information: in-app purchase history (we do not collect sensitive data like card numbers)
  • Advertising Identifiers: for personalized ads and ad performance tracking
  • Log and Analytics Data: app usage logs, crash reports, analytics via Firebase

2. How We Collect Information

  • Automatically when using the app
  • Through Firebase SDK integration
  • Via third-party login (Google and Apple)
  • From ad SDKs used in the app
  • Linked purchase data via Google/Apple platforms

3. Purpose of Information Use

  • To provide and manage the game service
  • To authenticate accounts via Google or Apple login
  • To process and restore in-app purchases
  • To deliver personalized ads and analyze ad performance
  • To handle customer inquiries and improve service quality
  • To monitor app performance and diagnose issues through Firebase

4. Advertising Networks

We use the following third-party ad networks to display advertisements in our app:

  • Google AdMob
  • Unity Ads
  • Vungle
  • One Admax

These providers may collect your advertising ID and usage patterns to show personalized ads.

5. Third-Party Login Services

  • Google Login
  • Apple Login

We collect limited information (user ID, email address if permitted, nickname) from these platforms solely for user identification and account linking purposes.

6. Retention and Deletion of Personal Information

We retain personal information only as long as needed for its intended purpose, or as required by applicable law. After that, the information will be safely deleted or anonymized.

7. Sharing of Personal Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • When we have your prior consent
  • When required by law or legal process
